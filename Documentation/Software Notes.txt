Hardware/Wiring assignments (updated 2/18/19)

Joystick 0:
	x-axis	not used
	y-axis	forward and back
	z-axis	turn
	slider	speed sensitivity

	buttons
		1-trigger		turbo mode
		2-thumb		toggle manual elevator mode
		3-top left		level 1 elevator
		4-top right		level 2 elevator
		5-top left		bottom level elevator
		6-top right		top level elevator

Joystick 1 (X-Box Controller):

	buttons
		1-A			get cargo ball (hold to activate, both A & Y off)
		2-B			hatch lock down (release hatch)
		3-X			hatch lock up (get hatch)
		4-Y yellow		spit out cargo ball (hold to activate, both A & Y off)
		5-top left front		arm down
		6-top right front	arm up

	gamepad joysticks
		left		not used
		right		manual elevator control	

	numerics
		POV-up		not used
		POV-down		not used
		POV-left		not used
		POV-right		not used
